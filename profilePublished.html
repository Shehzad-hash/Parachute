About

My journey as a child sleep consultant essentially began when my daughter Myla was born almost four years ago. After a short but very sweet start, she was diagnosed with colic and the next few blurry months were riddled with tears, self-doubt and soul crushing exhaustion. You might be going through something similar but there are a million other sleep challenges that children face.

I really needed help and just wanted someone to tell me what to do and how to do it. Confused by mixed advice from friends and books, I had reached my limit and decided to hire a sleep consultant to get me back on track and as Myla settled and got into her own groove, the whole family started to get more sleep…finally!

I would say I became fascinated as I learnt just how important a decent night’s sleep is to our children’s health, development and wellbeing.

4.9 Jade is a lifesaver!

Jade has been an absolute lifesaver in our sleep journey. We decided to contact a professional sleep trainer when my 6-month old son was waking up every 15-30mins during every nap and even during the night. The longest stretch he had ever slept since he was born was 2 hours! (And this only happened once or twice) During the three weeks we worked with Jade, she had been nothing but helpful, supportive, and professional. She guided and supported us everyday in helping my son get on a regular sleep and feeding schedule and the results have been nothing short of a miracle. In about two weeks time, my son was able to sleep through the night!! On his own, in his own crib, without any associations that he used to have, like sleeping by feeding, using a pacifier, holding and rocking him... I simply couldn't believe it. Even if he wakes up in the middle of the night, he is able to self-soothe back to sleep on his own. It really has been life-changing and our whole family can finally get some good night's sleep. It was an absolute pleasure to work with Jade, whose gentle yet firm approach was tailored to our situation and needs. She is detail-oriented, supportive, and always made sure we were comfortable with the steps during the whole process. She answered any and all questions we had so that we knew exactly how to handle any situation. I would definitely recommend Jade to all my friends!
